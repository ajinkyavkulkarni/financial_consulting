Financial Consulting — Legacy IT vs New-Age Tech Investment Thesis

CFA Institute Case Competition | Kriti 2026, IIT Guwahati · Feb–Mar 2026

A full-cycle equity research project building an investment thesis for two contrasting investor personas — wealth preservation (Legacy IT: TCS, Infosys) vs wealth accumulation (New-Age Tech: Eternal/Zomato). Covers macro sector diagnostics, bottom-up DCF valuations, and a strategic M&A proposal.

The Thesis in One Line
Legacy IT offers stable, dividend-backed compounding. New-Age Tech offers asymmetric upside — the sector crossed operational break-even for the first time in FY25, and the market hasn't fully priced the inflection.
